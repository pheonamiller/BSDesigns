About Me

I'm Bob Smith Graphic Designer

I am a freelance graphic designer based in Glasgow, Scotland, UK and have worked on illustration and design commissions around the globe. Andy Warhol and pop art sparked my passion for the world of design. I graduated from Glasgow Art School in 1993, attaining the Nesbitt Prize for Best Illustrator, the STV Prize for Best Film Stills Creator and the Penguin Prize for Best Book Designer.

Education

2017 - 2019 (University of Northumberland)

MA Design (Distance Learning Part-Time)

Whilst juggling my own freelance business full-time, I undertook this Masters programme. I learnt 3-D design, advanced graphic design, industrial design and design communication.

2015 - 2017 (Glasgow School of Art)

BDes (Hons)

Having attained the HND in Graphic Design, I was permitted to enter the third year of this degree course. I studied digital design, marketing, film stills and book illustration. During my studies, I attained the Nesbitt prize for best Film Stills and the Penguin Prize for Best Book Illustration.

2015 - 2017 (Glasgow Clyde College)

HND Graphic Design

I studied graphic design, animation, digital imaging, print making, DTP layout and typography.

Selected Notable Experiences

2017 - 2017

Internship - Scottish Television Studios (STV)

Having attained the STV prize for Best Film Stills Creator at undergraduate level, I was fortunate enough to secure a paid internship with Scotland's Film Studios. The programme lasted a year and I got to design stills for three famous Scottish Dramas such as 'River City,' 'Take the High Road' and 'Monarch of the Glen.'

2016 - 2016

Internship - Canongate Press, Edinburgh

This is Scotland's oldest independent book publishing House. It is renowned for printing unsual books and has won many accolades. I was fortunate enough to work on the award winning book 'Lanark' by Alasdair Gray.

2015 - 2015

Internship - G3 Creative Solutions

This is Scotland's largest graphic design company. Here I learnt logo and vector design.
